# Post-Sync QA (PSQA) Checklist

**Goal**: Confirm that LTIs, Respondus, gradebook configuration, and due dates are correct after a course sync.

> **Related**: See the [PSQA Form Mapping](psqa_form_mapping.md) for the exact form questions and response options.

---

## 1. Introduction

- [ ] **1. Course Code** — Enter the course code.
- [ ] **2. Status Change** — Change tracking list status to **"Under Post-Sync QA"**.

---

## 2. LTIs

- [ ] **3. LTI Links** — The course has the required LTIs and all links work correctly. Use the TeacherQA account and activate **Student Preview** to test.

---

## 3. Respondus

- [ ] **4. Respondus Activation** — Respondus is activated for the course *(N/A if course has no proctored tests)*.

---

## 4. Due Dates

- [ ] **5. Due Dates** — Due dates are adjusted for all assessments *(N/A if not applicable to program)*.

---

## 5. Gradebook

- [ ] **6. Grade Distribution** — Overall grade distribution matches the syllabus.
- [ ] **7. Gradebook Sorting** — All Gradebook items sorted correctly *(Final Grade → Overall Grade → COA → module activities in order)*.
- [ ] **8. Grade Schema** — "Pacific College Letter" grading schema matches the required parameters.

---

## 6. Wrap-Up

- [ ] **9. Time Log** — Add time spent on QA as a comment in the SharePoint list *(e.g., "PSQA time: 1h 40m")*.
- [ ] **10. Status Change** — Change tracking list status to **"Post-Sync QA Findings"** and add the findings link.
