# Program-Specific Quirks

This page documents exceptions and special rules that differ by program. Use this as a quick reference during any QA process (TQA, SQA, PSQA).

---

## TDAC

| Area | Quirk |
|------|-------|
| **Syllabus Location** | The syllabus is located in **Program Resources** instead of the course folder. |
| **Course Key Verbiage** | Course Key verbiage must be placed inside the "Start Here" module. |
| **Release Conditions** | Release conditions for Course Orientation Acknowledgment do **not** apply to TDAC. |
| **Due Dates** | The due dates are set on the Post-QA for TDAC. |

---

## Med CAN

| Area | Quirk |
|------|-------|
| **Course Key Verbiage** | Course Key verbiage must be placed inside the "Start Here" module. |

---

## Medical Science (MSN)

| Area | Quirk |
|------|-------|
| **Due Dates** | Due dates are set for **Monday of the following week**, not the current one. Discussion dates may also vary. |
| **Course Outline** | A course outline and schedule document must be provided. |
| **Release Conditions** | Exams in release conditions must open one week before the due date and close one week after (e.g., if due Sunday Aug 14, open Aug 7 and close Aug 21). |

---

## ATI

| Area | Quirk |
|------|-------|
| **Raw Links** | ATI resource links are excepted from the "no raw links" rule. |
